🎯 What is StudyAlert?

StudyAlert helps you track every assignment, exam, project and deadline in one place. It alerts you before things are due so you never miss anything important.

📖 How to Use — Step by Step
1
Add a Task

Fill the form on the left. Enter the task title (e.g. "Math Assignment"), pick a category, your subject, the due date and time, and a priority level. Hit Add Task.

2
Enable Notifications

Tap the 🔔 bell icon at the top. Allow permission when the browser asks. You'll get an alert 15 minutes before a task is due, and another alert at the exact due time.

3
View All Tasks in Calendar

Tap 📅 to open the calendar view. Days with tasks show coloured dots — like festivals on a calendar. Tap any day to see its tasks. Tap a task to open its full details.

4
Edit a Task

Tap the ✏️ pencil button on any task card. The form on the left fills in with the existing details. Make your changes and tap Save Changes. Tap ✕ Cancel Edit to abort.

5
Mark a Task Done

Tap the square checkbox on the left of any task card. It turns green with a ✓ and the task is crossed out. Tap it again to unmark it.

6
Delete a Task

Tap the 🗑️ bin icon on the task card. Confirm the popup. The task is permanently removed.

7
Search and Filter

Use the search box to find tasks by name or subject. Use the filter tabs — All, Pending, Done, Overdue — to quickly see only what matters.

8
Install on Your Phone

Open the app in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone), then use Add to Home Screen. It installs like a real app — no app store needed.

🎨 Calendar Dot Colours
Purple — Assignment Red — Exam / Quiz Teal — Project Orange — Lab / Practical Green — Reading Grey — Other / Completed
💡 Quick Tips
  • Tasks highlighted in orange are due within 3 days — act on them first.
  • Tasks highlighted in red are overdue.
  • Use Priority: High for exams and deadlines you cannot miss.
  • The daily morning notification lists everything due in the next 3 days — check it every day.
  • Press Escape anywhere to close any open panel.
  • Tap a task card to open its full detail view with a quick complete / edit / delete.
